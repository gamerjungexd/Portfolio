Travian Legends

Travian Legends is an expert MMO strategy war game. Embark on a thrilling adventure, an epic journey of exploration and conquest, where you can forge your army, harvest resources, and rise to total dominance by conquering empires and leading massive armies in epic, real-time multiplayer battles. Join global events, collect artifacts and epic weapons, manage your resources, and engage in tactical warfare to establish your empire's hegemony. Connect with millions of online players from around the world in real time, and together shape the destiny of Travian Legends.
