Purr in the Dark
(Work in progress - all information without warranty​)

"Purr in the dark" is an adventure stealth game in which the player, as a flying fox baby, must move through a dark attic without being detected by the cats sleeping there. To visualize both the environment and enemies, the player can use an echolocation system to determine the direction and distance of objects and threads.
